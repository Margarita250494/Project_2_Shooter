Shooting
This project was created for the Game Engines class.
The game is a shooter in the style of Aim Lab, designed to help players improve their accuracy and tracking skills.

About the Game
Objective: Test and refine your aiming skills through challenging scenarios.
Style: Simple and focused, with mechanics aimed at precision and practice.
Purpose: Enhance accuracy and tracking while offering a straightforward and engaging gameplay experience.



Features
Multiple weapons to simulate different shooting styles.
Clean and minimalist UI for seamless gameplay.
Smooth mechanics focused on precision and responsiveness.


How to Play
Select a difficulty level.
Select a weapon from the UI.
Aim and shoot at the targets.
Check your score at the end.
